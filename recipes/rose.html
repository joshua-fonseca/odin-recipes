Sauce Rosee

Credit: recipe

Description

Try rose sauce with tortellini pasta and shrimp, and serve with a Caesar salad, if desired. Restaurants beware: this sauce can top you!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons minced green bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 7 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon chicken soup base
  • 6 fluid ounces heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup white wine

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; stir in tortellini and return to a gentle boil. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tortellini float to the top and filling is hot, about 4 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir basil, thyme, oregano, parsley, and red pepper flakes into hot oil.
  4. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until onions are translucent, about 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce and bring to a boil. Stir in sugar and chicken bouillon until dissolved.
  6. Reduce heat and stir in cream and wine; bring close to a boil, stirring constantly. Gently stir in tortellini and serve.
